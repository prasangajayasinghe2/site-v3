Project Overview
A parametric enclosure designed to mount directly onto standard DIN rails, providing a secure and accessible housing for electronic control modules. The box was modelled in SolidWorks with precise attention to the IEC 60715 rail profile, ensuring a reliable clip-fit without play or rattle.
Design considerations included internal partitioning for component separation, ventilation slot placement for thermal management, and a removable lid with retaining features for easy field access. The geometry was finalised for FDM 3D printing with optimised wall thicknesses and self-tapping screw boss details throughout.
